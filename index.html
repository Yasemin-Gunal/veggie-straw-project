THE BIG IDEA

"SECRETLY HEALTHY"

While never explicitly saying it outright, Garden Veggie Straws implies that they are a healthier replacement to junk food.

This means the product must scratch that junk food itch, in other words it must still taste like junk food. The idea of "secretly" healthy is that this is a healthy snack that doesn't taste like a healthy snack.

SLOGAN

Snack better, live better.

BRAND STORY

Tricking your kid into eating healthy is a struggle for many parents. How can we encourage healthy habits in secret?
