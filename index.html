RE-BRAND PROJECT

I was tasked to create an entirely new brand for the product Garden Veggie Straws.

THE BIG IDEA

"SECRETLY HEALTHY"

Garden Veggie Straws are going off of the idea that they are a healthier replacement for your average junk food snack (mainly potato chips.)

This means the product must scratch that junk food itch- in other words it must still taste like junk food. The idea of "secretly" healthy is that this is a healthy snack that doesn't taste like a healthy snack, hence the secrecy.

BRAND STORY

Tricking your kid into eating healthy is a struggle for many parents. Lets can we encourage healthy habits in secret.
