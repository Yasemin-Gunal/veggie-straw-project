YASEMIN EMIRA GUNAL

I picked up a pen age-minus-six years ago, and never put it down since then. Creation is something I never had to think about because it was never not a part of me. Over the years and up until now I have dabbled in it all. From tying rocks to sticks with grass, to creating stories at the tip of my stylus, I always wanted to do it all.

I am currently a 2nd year student at University of Waterloo, majoring in its Global Business Digital Arts program. My biggest inspiration to create are the stories of everyone and everything around me. Everyone has a story, whether it be of their life, or one perfectly crafted to tell. Books, cartoons, video-games, words-of-mouth; all ways to convey a new idea that I desperately wish to understand and use in my own work.

Now that you know me we should connect, so you can return the favour!
